Select Game Mode

x01 Mode

Everyone starts on 101, 201 or 301 and races to EXACTLY zero. Three darts a turn. Straight In means every dart counts from the first throw; Double In means your scoring doesn't start until you hit a double. You must FINISH on a double that lands you exactly on zero (the 50 bull counts). Go below zero, land on 1, or reach zero without a double and the turn is a BUST — your score goes back to where it started.

Starting Rule

Killer Mode

Each player throws with their NON-DOMINANT hand until they land on a free number — however many darts it takes. That number is theirs. Claim in any order (lucky pick, first to arrive) — the match is then played in the order numbers were claimed. Hit OPPONENT numbers to give them strikes. Hit YOUR OWN number to remove strikes from yourself. First to 5 strikes is ELIMINATED. Last player standing wins.

Shanghai Mode

Each player throws with their NON-DOMINANT hand until they land on a free number (1–20) — however many darts it takes. That number is theirs for the WHOLE game, and claiming is the only non-dominant throwing of the match. Claim in any order (lucky pick, first to arrive). The match is then played in the order numbers were claimed. Hit a SINGLE, a DOUBLE and a TRIPLE of your own number, in any order, to finish. First four to finish take First, Second, Third and Fourth.
