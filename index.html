Select Game Mode

X:001 Mode

Everyone starts on 101, 201 or 301 and races to EXACTLY zero. Three darts a turn. Straight In means every dart counts from the first throw; Double In means your scoring doesn't start until you hit a double. You must FINISH on a double that lands you exactly on zero (the 50 bull counts). Go below zero, land on 1, or reach zero without a double and the turn is a BUST — your score goes back to where it started.

Select Game Type

Starting Rule

Places

How many finishing places before the game ends. 1 is a classic single winner; pick more and play continues for Second, Third… If only one player is left unplaced, they take the last place automatically.

Killer Mode

Each player throws with their NON-DOMINANT hand until they land on a free number — however many darts it takes. That number is theirs. Claim in any order (lucky pick, first to arrive) — the match is then played in the order numbers were claimed. Hit OPPONENT numbers to give them strikes. Hit YOUR OWN number to remove strikes from yourself. First to 5 strikes is ELIMINATED. Last player standing wins.

Places

How many finishing places get an official rank. Places are awarded in reverse elimination order — the winner is First, the last player eliminated is Second, the one before them Third, and so on. Everyone outside the chosen places stays unplaced and can go into the Bonus Round.

Shanghai Mode

Each player throws with their NON-DOMINANT hand until they land on a free number (1–20) — however many darts it takes. That number is theirs for the WHOLE game, and claiming is the only non-dominant throwing of the match. Claim in any order (lucky pick, first to arrive). The match is then played in the order numbers were claimed. Hit a SINGLE, a DOUBLE and a TRIPLE of your own number, in any order, to finish.

Places

How many finishing places before the game ends. Play continues for First, Second… in the order players finish. If only one player is left unplaced, they take the last place automatically.
